About Project

This study conducts a comparative analysis of traditional machine learning for intrusion detection in self-organizing networks. The methodologies evaluated include Random Forest, Decision Tree,K-Best feature selection, and K-Means clustering. The backend implementation utilizes Python, while the frontend is developed with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. K-Best feature selection and K-Means clustering are employed to enhance data preprocessing and clustering efficiency.
